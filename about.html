Who I Am

The person behind the test cases.

👩🏾‍💻

I'm Oluwadarasimi Olowe — a graduate assistant who took on this internship to find out if QA was the right fit for me. It turned out to be more than a fit. I found something I genuinely love.

I'm the kind of person who asks "what happens if this goes wrong?" before anyone else thinks to. I care about how software performs, how users experience it, and what breaks before it reaches them. I got into QA because I love finding the cracks that no one else notices — identifying issues, understanding why they happen, and helping build something better because of it.

I'm detail-oriented, feedback-driven, and I take criticism as a tool, not a threat. I'm an introvert — which means I observe a lot and speak when it matters.
